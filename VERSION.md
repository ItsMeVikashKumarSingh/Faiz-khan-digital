# Version History

## 0.4.0 (2026-02-13)
- **Legal Compliance:** Added Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, and Refund Policy pages.
- **UI Enhancements:**
    - Standardized `Button`, `Input`, `Textarea`, `Card`, and `Toast` components.

- **Functionality:**
    - Integrated Web3Form for contact inquiries.
